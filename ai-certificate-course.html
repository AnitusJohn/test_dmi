PROGRAM

CERTIFICATE COURSE IN AI

Upskilling Educators for the AI Era

Teach With AI is a practical and forward-thinking training program designed specifically for educators who want to understand and apply Artificial Intelligence in their teaching practices.

As AI tools become more common in classrooms, this program equips teachers with the skills and confidence to use AI responsibly, creatively, and effectively. Whether you're a beginner or tech-savvy, Teaching with AI offers step-by-step guidance on how to bring AI into everyday teaching.

This program is ideal for teachers looking to enhance their digital literacy, personalize student learning, and stay relevant in a rapidly changing educational environment.

Benefits of Teach With AI for participants include:

  • Gaining a clear understanding of Artificial Intelligence and its applications in education
  • Hands-on training with AI tools for lesson planning, content creation, and student assessment
  • Learning how to use AI to personalize learning and support diverse student needs
  • Exploring tools like ChatGPT for writing prompts, feedback, and idea generation
  • Enhancing classroom productivity through automation and smart assistance
  • Understanding the ethical and responsible use of AI in schools
  • Preparing students for the future by modeling effective and safe AI use
  • Becoming a tech-savvy teacher ready for 21st-century classrooms

Teaching with AI empowers teachers to embrace the future of education with confidence and creativity.
