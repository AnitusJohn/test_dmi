Course

Diploma in X- Ray Technology

The Radiology or X-ray Technician course trains students to work as radiology technicians in radiology departments of hospitals, diagnostic centres and clinics. After undergoing training in basic human anatomy and physiology, students are taught to handle X-ray equipment, develop exposed X-ray films using both wet development and computerised X-ray machines. As part of the course, they are given in-depth training of CT Scan, MRI and ultrasonography equipment.
